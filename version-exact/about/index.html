Meet Your Insider

After decades representing hotels, I started Stellina Connections to do what I love most: put the client first. For 25 years, I sat on the hotel side of the negotiation table. I saw firsthand how corporate shifts often pulled focus away from relationships — and I wanted to change that. I launched Stellina to offer a new vantage point: big-agency capability paired with the personal dedication of a true partner.

A Global Life, A Global Perspective

Hospitality isn’t just my profession; it’s who I am. I was born in Italy, but thanks to my father’s career with Alitalia, the world quickly became my classroom. I grew up everywhere — New Jersey, Tokyo, Frankfurt, Rio de Janeiro, Bangkok — before returning to the U.S. for college.
Living across continents didn’t just teach me geography; it taught me how to connect with people, understand nuance, and appreciate the magic of travel. Along the way, I became fluent in Italian and Portuguese, and speak conversational Spanish, which allows me to build trust and navigate international partnerships with ease.

The Professional Journey

My career officially began when a New York hotel sponsored my visa, recognizing my drive long before I had a résumé to match. From there, I grew through the industry, eventually overseeing sales strategies for hundreds of hotels and DMCs. I learned exactly how hotels operate, how they price, and where they have room to move.
Today, I use that insider knowledge to advocate for you — protecting your interests, maximizing your budget, and uncovering opportunities others might miss.

My Commitment to You

When you hire Stellina Connections, you don’t get a junior associate — you get me. I am hands-on, deeply invested, and fully dedicated to the clients who trust me with their events. Whether we’re negotiating a complex contract or discovering the perfect property in a crowded market, I am in your corner every step of the way.

You're in Control

Whether you want to be deeply involved or check in only at key moments, our process flexes around your style. Collaboration on your terms, progress without pressure.

And a Final Note

Known for my small stature — and even bigger commitment — I’m fiercely loyal to my clients and always go above and beyond.
